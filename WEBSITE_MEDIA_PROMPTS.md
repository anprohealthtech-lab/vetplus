# Vetplus Diagnostics - Veterinary Media Prompts Guide

Use these highly descriptive prompts in your AI generation engine (like Nanobanana, Midjourney, Genmo, or Runway) to generate the perfect, consistent graphical assets for the new Vetplus Diagnostics public website.

All images should follow the **Vetplus Brand Guidelines**:
- **Core Colors:** Clean Clinical Whites, Deep Trust Blues (`#1a3a5c`), and Soft Teals/Greens for accents.
- **Vibe:** Highly professional veterinary care, compassionate toward animals, technologically advanced, spotless, and trustworthy.
- **Subjects:** Veterinary professionals, advanced diagnostic equipment, and calm, happy pets (like Golden Retrievers, Labradors, or domestic cats).
- **Lighting:** Bright, natural daylight, soft laboratory ambient lighting, optimistic.

---

## 📸 Image Prompts

### 1. Home Page Hero Image (Main Background)
**Placement:** The massive background image at the top of the Home page.
**Prompt:** 
> A cinematic, wide-angle shot of a state-of-the-art veterinary pathology laboratory. In the center, a friendly, compassionate veterinarian in a premium white lab coat with a subtle teal stethoscope around her neck is gently but professionally petting a calm, happy Golden Retriever sitting on a stainless steel medical examination table. In the softly blurred background, a sleek automated blood analyzer machine with glowing blue LED lights. Bright, natural sunlight streaming through large windows. High resolution, 8k, photorealistic, architectural medical photography style, soft depth of field. Use a color palette of clinical white, deep blue, and teal.

### 2. About Us - Lab Technology
**Placement:** Alongside the "Modern Technology" card or mission statement.
**Prompt:**
> Close-up macro photography of an advanced veterinary medical robotic testing machine processing neat, sterile test tubes labeled with subtle paw-print motifs. The machine is high-tech, sleek, and metallic with subtle glowing teal indicator lights. The environment is impeccably clean, representing precision veterinary diagnostics, speed, and 100% accuracy. Crisp focus, 8k resolution, professional veterinary medical photography.

### 3. About Us - The Expert Team
**Placement:** Alongside the "Expert Team" card.
**Prompt:**
> A candid, professional portrait of two dedicated veterinary pathologists discussing results while looking at a glowing monitor displaying an animal's digital health report. They are dressed in premium white lab coats over smart casual clothing. They look highly experienced, empathetic, and focused on animal welfare. The background is a gently blurred, bright, modern veterinary clinic office. Soft cinematic lighting, 8k, photorealistic, conveying immense trust to pet owners.

### 4. Home Collection Background / Hero
**Placement:** The top banner for the Home Collection page.
**Prompt:**
> A warm, empathetic shot of a certified veterinary phlebotomist arriving at a beautiful, sunlit residential living room to collect a health sample from a pet. The technician, wearing clean professional medical scrubs (teal color) and a branded id badge, is smiling and respectfully holding a sleek, sterile medical collection kit. A comfortable pet owner sits on the sofa with a calm, well-groomed tabby cat resting in their lap. It conveys utmost convenience, hygiene, and stress-free at-home veterinary healthcare. Photorealistic, soft morning light, 8k, highly detailed.

## 🎥 Video Prompts

### 1. Hero Promotional Video (Background Loop)
**Placement:** Can be used as a moving background on the Homepage Hero section or as a promo video on social media.
**Duration:** 5 - 10 seconds (Loopable)
**Prompt:**
> Smooth, cinematic slow-motion tracking shot. We start focused on a pristine, high-tech blood analyzer machine cleanly moving a medical test tube. The camera slowly pans right to reveal a confident, smiling veterinarian in a crisp white coat gently comforting a cute, calm Beagle puppy on an examination table while reviewing digital data on a transparent, futuristic tablet that emits a soft blue light. The lighting is bright, sterile yet optimistic, with natural sunbeams piercing through the lab windows. The movement is extremely stable, corporate, and elegant. 4k resolution, 60fps, veterinary commercial style.

### 2. Home Collection "How it Works" Short
**Placement:** Above the "3-Step Process" on the Home Collection page.
**Duration:** 5 seconds
**Prompt:**
> Gentle, slow-motion shot of a smiling veterinary diagnostic worker in clean teal medical scrubs carefully snapping shut a modern, sterile sample transport cooler box in a warmly lit, elegant home living room. A happy dog wags its tail gently in the foreground. The worker turns and gives a reassuring nod to the camera. Soft cinematic lighting, high-end veterinary commercial aesthetic, feeling of safety for pets, speed, and premium service. 4k resolution.
