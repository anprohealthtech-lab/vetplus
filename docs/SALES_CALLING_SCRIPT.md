## PART 2: DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (1-2 minutes)

Ask 2-3 of these to understand the lab:

1. "Sir, how many tests do you process daily on average?"
2. "Are you currently using any software, or is everything manual / register-based?"
3. "Do you outsource any tests to reference labs?"
4. "How do you share reports with patients currently - printed, WhatsApp, email?"
5. "Do you have collection centers or multiple locations?"
6. "Do you work with any referring doctors or hospitals on a sharing basis?"
7. "What is your biggest pain point today - report delays, billing errors, result re-entry?"

> **Thermal Slip / Instrument Screen Reading:**
> "You know how your technician types results from the analyzer screen or thermal printer slip? With CloudLIMS, you just take a photo of the slip or analyzer screen with your phone. Our AI reads the values, maps them to the correct parameters, and fills the report - automatically. No typing."

## PART 4: OBJECTION HANDLING

### "We already use software"

> "Sir, that's great that you're already digital. Can I ask - does your current software have AI result entry from photos? Voice input? WhatsApp integration? Most labs that switch to us do so for these AI features that traditional software doesn't offer. We can even import your existing data."

### "We are too small / only 20-30 tests a day"

> "Sir, actually we designed CloudLIMS for labs of your size. You don't need IT staff or a server. Everything runs on your browser - even on a phone. And with AI doing the data entry, you actually need fewer people to manage more tests."

### "It's too expensive / what's the cost?"

> "Sir, our pricing is subscription-based - you pay monthly, no big upfront cost. Most labs recover the cost in the first month itself through time saved and fewer errors. Can I share the pricing over WhatsApp after we discuss your requirements?"

### "We don't trust cloud / our data will be unsafe"

> "Sir, your data is stored in Supabase - which runs on AWS infrastructure - the same cloud used by banks and hospitals worldwide. All data is encrypted, access is role-based, and only your lab staff can see your data. We also provide Row-Level Security - even within your lab, a technician can only see their location's data."

### "We tried software before, it was too complicated"

> "Sir, that's exactly why we built voice input and AI. Your technician doesn't need to type - just speak or take a photo. The learning curve is minimal. We provide onboarding support and training as part of the package."

### "Can we see a demo first?"

> "Absolutely sir. I can arrange a 30-minute live demo where we show you the actual system with your lab's tests. Shall we schedule it for [tomorrow / day after]? I'll also send you a short video on WhatsApp in the meantime."
